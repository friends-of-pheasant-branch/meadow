Make Your TDS Homepage Your Own

Step 2

Simply click on the "Personalize" link on tds.net. Customize your homepage to suit your preferences and interests; news, sports, local weather, games, and much more.

TDS Email

Step 3

TDS Email brings you the best email solution available today.

You should have already received your email user name and password. If you have forgotten your user name or password, please call 800-605-1962.

To access your email, go to TDS.net, click “Check Email” or “Preview Email” and enter your user name and password. It’s that easy!

If you’d like to use your email program to get TDS email (i.e. Outlook, etc.), you can learn how here.
